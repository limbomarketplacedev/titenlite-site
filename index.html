What is Tite-n-Lite?

Tite-n-Lite is the world’s first flashlight designed to work with just one battery of any size. Whether it’s an AA, AAA, C, or even a D-cell, Tite-n-Lite adapts instantly — giving you light when you need it most.

No more worrying about the “right” batteries. Tite-n-Lite is built for emergencies, camping, everyday use, and disaster readiness. It’s compact, durable, and always ready to shine — even if all you have is a single leftover battery.
